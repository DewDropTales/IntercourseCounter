Methodology

Population Data: Real-time world population from World Bank via CORS proxy with live growth applied.

Intercourse Frequency Calculation:

  • Sexually active population: 40% of total (ages 15-64, adjusted)
  • Average frequency: 78 acts per person per year (global studies)
  • Regional and age variations factored in

Conception Rate: Based on global contraceptive prevalence and method-specific failure rates.
