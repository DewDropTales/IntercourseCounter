Methodology

Population Data: Real-time world population from World Bank API with growth rate applied.

Intercourse Frequency Calculation:

  • Sexually active population: 40% of total (ages 15-64, adjusted for activity rates)
  • Average frequency: 78 acts per person per year (based on global studies)
  • Regional variations and age demographics factored in

Conception Rate Calculation:

  • Contraceptive usage: 64% global rate (varies by region: 75% developed, 55% developing)
  • Failure rates by method:
    • Sterilization: 0.5% failure rate (33% of users)
    • IUD: 0.8% failure rate (14% of users)
    • Pill/Injection: 7% typical failure rate (18% of users)
    • Condoms: 13% typical failure rate (20% of users)
    • Traditional methods: 24% failure rate (15% of users)
  • No contraception: 36% of sexually active population, 85% annual pregnancy probability
  • Natural conception rate: 20-25% per cycle for unprotected intercourse

Counter Updates: The live counter simulates real-time activity with smooth animation based on calculated acts per second.
